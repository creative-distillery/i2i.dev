In early 2018, the North Carolina Council of Community Programs will become i2i Center for Integrative Health.

With North Carolina’s Medicaid program transitioning to a whole person managed care system designed to care for both physical and behavioral health, we’ll face new challenges. These new challenges will demand new solutions.

That’s where we come in.

i2i’s mission will build on the NC Council’s core strength--working across the behavioral health/IDD community to create solutions. We need to bring together all of managed care’s stakeholders to find common ground--fast.

We’ll be rolling-out our first issue collaborative this Spring. But we’ll have lots of challenges, and you can help. Tell us what you think is most important as North Carolina transitions to whole-person managed care.

Thank you! Please bookmark this page and stay tuned for more updates early next year.

Mission

To foster collaborative and evidence-based initiatives  for improving the quality and efficacy of the behavioral health, intellectual and developmental disabilities, and substance use care and support services systems  within a comprehensive system of whole person care.

Vision

To be the trusted convener of the broad coalition of perspectives engaged and invested in improving the quality of care and quality of life for North Carolinians through strategic partnerships, rigorous research and education, and exploration of best practices.
